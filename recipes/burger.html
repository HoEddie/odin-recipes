Burger Recipe

Hamburger

Description

A juicy hamburger with lettuce, tomatoes, and a side of fries - perfect for a weeknight meal.

I got this recipe from my Uncle who was a menace at the BBQ.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tsp hot sauce
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3/4 cup breadcrumbs

Recipe

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, hot sauce and bread crumbs
  3. Shape mixture into 6 patties
  4. Grill patties over medium heat for 10-20 minutes
