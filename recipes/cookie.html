Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

These were fantastic cookies, without a doubt. I did lessen the white sugar to 1/2 cup and increase the brown sugaar to 1 1/2 cups for a richer flavour. I also recommend using vanilla paste

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F or 175 degrees C
  2. Cream together the butter,sugar, and brown sugar until smooth
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time
  4. Stir in vanilla
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water
  6. Add batter along with salt
  7. Stir in flour and chocolate
  8. Drop batter into ungreased pans
  9. Bake for about 10 min in preheated oven
